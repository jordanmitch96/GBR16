ABOUT | GOODBYE REBEL 16


Goodbye Rebel 16 founders Anthony and Billy met at a small club in Southern California in 1987. Both at the club for a battle of the bands, the two learned that they had a shared interest in music. Anthony, a singer, and Billy, a guitarist, they soon recruited fellow musicians Bonnie Blue, drums, and Flea, bass. The next year, at the same club, they performed at the battle of the bands.

They lost.


But, they won the attention of an indie record label and Goodbye Rebel 16 started their first tour in 1989, 2 years after Anthony and Billy met.

A blend of rock, funk, and punk, Goodbye Rebel 16 spans musical genres with an eclectic sound.


Join Goodbye Rebel 16 on our European Tour starting in London and ending in Madrid.
